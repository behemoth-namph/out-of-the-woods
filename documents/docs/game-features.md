# Game Features Overview — Out of the Woods

| feature | one-line summary | trigger | details |
|---|---|---|---|
| `base_game` | 25 fixed paylines pay 3/4/5 identical symbols left-to-right from reel 1; Wild substitutes for all but Scatter. | every base spin | [[math-and-rtp]] |
| `multiplier_wild_stack` | Multiplier Wild lands only as a full-reel vertical stack with 1×/2×/3×/4× multipliers bottom-to-top. | a full reel lands covered in Multiplier Wilds | [[multiplier_wild_stack-mechanics]] |
| `wild_respin` | A win on a spin containing a Multiplier Wild reel auto-respins; the stack shifts down one row (or random 2–10 if the golden-rim would exit) and every multiplier +1×, repeating while wins continue. | winning combination on a spin with a Multiplier Wild reel | [[wild_respin-mechanics]] |
| `free_spins` | 3/4/5 Scatters award 10/15/20 free spins on special reels; the highest multiplier reached persists and seeds later stacks. | 3, 4 or 5 Scatters | [[free_spins-mechanics]] |
| `ante_bet` | Special Bets: Normal 25×, Ante Bet 125× (12× higher FS chance), Super Spin 250× (one guaranteed stack per spin, Free Spins disabled). | player selects a special bet | [[ante_bet-mechanics]] |
| `buy_feature` | Buy Free Spins for 100× bet, or Super Free Spins for 500× bet (first stack 10×/11×/12×/13×). | purchase from the Buy Feature panel | [[buy_feature-mechanics]] |
| `max_win_cap` | Win capped at 10,000× bet; round ends immediately and remaining features are forfeited when reached. | feature total reaches 10,000× bet | [[max_win_cap-mechanics]] |
