# UI & Controls — Out of the Woods

Authored from the paytable "HOW TO PLAY" pages (`[t22]`,`[t24]`,`[t25]`), the main-screen layout (`[t07]`), and the live play-through. No math figures here.

## Controls
| control | function | states | open/close flow | evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Spin button (center) | start a spin / stop autoplay | idle / spinning / (stop) | click to spin; during autoplay acts as stop | `[t07]`,`[t26]`,`[t69]` |
| Turbo Spin | fast blur-drop spins | on / off; also **HOLD SPACE** for turbo | toggle in Autoplay settings, or hold Space | `[t07]`,`[t35]`,`[t41]` |
| Quick Spin | shorter spin animation | on / off | toggle in Autoplay settings | `[t34]` |
| Bet – / Bet + | decrease / increase total bet | min…max (steps not transcribed) | click ± on bottom bar; Bet Menu on info page | `[t07]`,`[t24]` |
| Autoplay | run N auto-spins | opens Autoplay Settings panel | click autoplay icon → panel → START; stop via spin/stop control | `[t34]`,`[t35]`,`[t70]` |
| Info (i) | open GAME RULES / paytable (7 pages) | pages 1/7…7/7 | click (i) → paginate → close; paytable reached on page 1 | `[t08]`–`[t25]` |
| Menu / Settings | Intro Screen toggle, Ambient, Sound FX, Game History | open / closed | click menu icon → settings list | `[t07]`,`[t24]` |
| History / reload icon | game/spin history | open / closed | click history icon | `[t07]`,`[t24]` |
| Sound (Sound FX / Ambient) | audio toggles | on / off each | inside Settings menu | `[t24]` |
| Buy Feature | purchase FS or Super FS entry | 2 options panel | click "BUY FEATURE — 2 OPTIONS" → panel | `[t07]`,`[t70]` |
| Special Bets | select Normal / Ante / Super Spin | 2 options panel | click "SPECIAL BETS — 2 OPTIONS" → panel | `[t07]` |
| Balance / Win display | show credit + last win | live readout | bottom bar (Credit, Bet, Win) | `[t07]`,`[t28]` |

**Autoplay stop conditions offered:** Autoplay Settings exposes Turbo Spin, Quick Spin, **Skip Screens** (pre-checked), and a **Number of Autospins slider** (observed at 100, likely the max) with a **START AUTOPLAY (N)** button (`[t34]`). Explicit loss-limit / single-win-limit stop conditions were **not observed** in the panel `[t34]`.
